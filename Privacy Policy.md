1. Information We Collect

    Message Data: The Bot processes messages in real-time to identify TikTok links. Once a message is checked, it is discarded. We do not store the content of your messages.

    User/Server IDs: Discord IDs are processed only to execute the reply and are not saved in a permanent database.

2. Data Storage and Retention

    No Database: This Bot does not maintain a database of user profiles or message history.

    Technical Logs: Our server generates standard technical logs for troubleshooting. These logs are automatically deleted after 30 days.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing

The Bot uses third-party "fixer" domains to enable video previews. Clicking or viewing a fixed link may expose your IP address to those third-party services and TikTok, as with any external link.
